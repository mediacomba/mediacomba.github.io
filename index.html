  • Portraits Of The Indian Flower Men Project by Ken Hermann

    It's a unique series of portraits of the flower sellers of Malik Ghat Flower Market in Kolkata, India

    Flowers are a hugely important part of Indian culture, used in everything from temple rituals to festivals and parties – and Malik Ghat Flower Market in Kolkata is the largest of its kind in India. Two thousand sellers gather daily to peddle their blooms. The streets are lined with bowls and knots of flowers. Every colour, species and fragrance fused together, overwhelming the senses.

  • The Flower Men book will be a high quality photo book containing 40 beautiful portraits of the flower sellers

    The layout is minimalistic and modern which enhances the strong images. The book will go to print as soon as the Kickstarter ends. It will be published by the renowned German Kehrer Verlag and design is done by the Danish design agency Wonderland
